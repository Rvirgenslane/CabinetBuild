## **Project: Double-Stacked Shaker Cabinets**
**Style:** Full-overlay Shaker doors with a Maple face frame and Plywood carcass.

### **1. Overall Dimensions (Per Unit)**
* **Total Width:** 35 1/2"
* **Total Height:** 38 3/4"
* **Total Depth:** 12 1/4" (12.25")

### **2. Carcass Construction (The Box)**
* **Material:** 3/4" Plywood (Light Brown).
* **Joints:** Top and Bottom panels sit into the Side panels via **1/8" deep internal rabbets**.
* **Backer:** 1/4" Plywood panel set at the very rear of the box.
* **Interior:** One adjustable shelf per unit sitting on **1/4" shelf pins**.

### **3. Face Frame**
* **Material:** 3/4" Thick Maple (Dark Brown).
* **Design:** A "border" frame that sits flush on the front edges of the plywood box to hide the plys.

### **4. Shaker Door Specs (Two Doors per Unit)**
* **Door Size:** 17 9/16" Wide x 38 5/8" High.
* **Door Frame:** 2 1/4" Wide Maple (3/4" thick).
* **Door Insert:** 14 1/16" Wide x 35 1/8" High. 
* **Insert Detail:** 1/4" thick panel with a **1/2" tongue** that nests into the maple frame.
* **Reveal:** Since the cabinet is 38 3/4" tall and the doors are 38 5/8", there is a **1/16" gap** at the top and bottom.
